# Donate

Conjoyn is free, and every feature stays free — nothing is locked behind a payment.

It's built and maintained by one person. If Conjoyn saved you time and you'd like to help keep it maintained — covering the Apple developer account, signing, and the hours that go into new cameras and fixes — a donation is genuinely appreciated. It's entirely optional, and the app works exactly the same either way.

[**Donate to support Conjoyn**](https://apps.lucesumbrarum.com/donate.html?app=conjoyn)

The link opens the support page in your browser. Thank you for considering it.
