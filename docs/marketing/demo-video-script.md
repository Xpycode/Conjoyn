# Conjoyn — Demo Video Script & Shot List (60–90s)

> The single most-reused marketing asset: landing-page hero, Product Hunt, Reddit, YouTube short,
> forum embeds. Goal: in under 90 seconds, make a drone pilot think *"oh — that's exactly my problem,
> and it's one drag."* Show the product doing the work; narrate the outcome, not the tech. Silent-friendly
> (most viewers watch muted) — so on-screen text must carry the story on its own.

---

## Principles

- **Show, don't tell.** Real footage, real split files, real merge. No slides.
- **Problem in the first 3 seconds.** People scroll fast — lead with the pain, not the logo.
- **One idea per shot.** Don't cram.
- **Captions always on.** Assume no audio.
- **End on the differentiator** (SRT telemetry) + a clear CTA.
- Keep it **60–75s** for social; a 90s cut is fine for the landing page.

## Shot list (what to actually capture)

1. **Beauty drone shot** (4–6s) — your own footage; sets the "real pilot" tone.
2. **Finder window** with genuine split files: `DJI_0001…0004.MP4` + matching `.SRT`. Make it look real
   (messy, lots of files).
3. **The FFmpeg/Terminal "pain" shot** (2–3s) — a real `list.txt` + command, scrolling. Keep it brief;
   you're contrasting, not teaching.
4. **Screen recording of Conjoyn** at high resolution (record at 2x retina, export crisp):
   - empty window
   - the drag-drop (do it slowly and deliberately; this is the hero frame)
   - the auto-grouping result, including a variant kept separate
   - the lossless join + output file
   - the merged playback with telemetry overlay in sync
   - watch-folder picking up a new flight
5. **End card** — logo, tagline, URL, download button.

---

## Production notes

- **Record clean.** Hide personal data, use a neutral wallpaper, default cursor, no clutter in the menu bar.
- **Pace the drag-drop.** The drop is the emotional payoff — give it a beat, maybe a subtle zoom.
- **Captions burned in**, high contrast, readable on mobile.
- **Music:** light, modern, low. Duck it if there's VO.
- **Export multiple cuts from the same project:**
  - 16:9 ~75s — landing page + YouTube + Product Hunt
  - 9:16 ~30–45s — YouTube Shorts / vertical, trimmed to shots 1, 5–8, 10
  - 6–10s silent loop (just shots 1 + 5 + 7) — landing-page hero background / forum embeds
- **First frame matters** — set the thumbnail to the split-files-pile or the drag moment, not the logo.
